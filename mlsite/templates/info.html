Overview of the Data

This dataset includes scores from three exams and a variety of personal, social, and economic factors that have interaction effects upon them.

Our goal is to predict whether a student will pass the tests or not.

We took under consideration the following arguments:

  1. Subjects Gender
  2. Subjects Parents level of education
  3. Subjects Kind (which group does the subject belong)
  4. Does the subject have eaten lunch?
  5. Did the subject took a preparation course for the exams?
